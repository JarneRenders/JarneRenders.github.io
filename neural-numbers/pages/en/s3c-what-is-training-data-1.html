Training data is the basis for a neural network to do what you want. It is usually a large set of sample inputs combined with the desired outputs. Often a large part of the training data is used to train the network, while the remaining part is used to test the network afterward, checking its accuracy.

Training data is very valuable, and luckily there are open databases, like the MNIST database of handwritten digits that we used for this exhibit.
